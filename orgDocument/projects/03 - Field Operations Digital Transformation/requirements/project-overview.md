# Field Operations Digital Transformation - Project Overview

## Project Identifier
PRJ-03

## Objective
Transform field operations through digital tools and processes to modernize service delivery, improve data collection, and enhance operational visibility.

## Scope
- Digital workflow implementation
- Mobile technology adoption
- Real-time data collection and reporting
- Integration with back-office systems
- Field personnel training and change management

## Key Stakeholders
- Field Operations Teams
- IT Department
- Operations Management
- Training and Development
- Change Management

## Success Criteria
- 100% digital workflow adoption
- Real-time field data availability
- Improved operational decision-making
- Enhanced field-to-office communication

## Related Organizational Processes
- [Field Management Process](../../orgModel/main.md)
- [Digital Integration Process](../../orgModel/main.md)

## Project Timeline
- Phase 1: Requirements and Technology Assessment (Q1 2026)
- Phase 2: Digital Platform Development (Q2-Q3 2026)
- Phase 3: Pilot Implementation and Rollout (Q4 2026)

## Key Deliverables
- Technology assessment report
- Digital transformation roadmap
- Mobile application development
- Training curriculum
- Change management plan
- Success metrics framework
