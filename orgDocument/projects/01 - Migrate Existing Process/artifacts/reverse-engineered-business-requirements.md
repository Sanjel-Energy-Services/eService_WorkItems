**A:** If you win the bid, what are the next steps?

**A:** How do you handle reporting and invoicing?

## Key Business Requirements (from Interview)

1. **Service Invitation Handling:** Ability to receive and track client invitations for cementing services.
2. **Proposal & Bidding:** System to prepare, submit, and manage service proposals and bids.
3. **Bid Result Processing:** Mechanism to record and act on client bidding decisions.
4. **Service Request Management:** Workflow to initiate and track cementing service requests post-bid.
5. **Service Execution:** Tools and processes for planning, mobilizing, and performing cementing services at well sites.
6. **Reporting & Invoicing:** Automated generation and delivery of service reports and invoices to clients.
7. **Payment Tracking:** Monitor client payments and outstanding invoices.
8. **Post Job Analysis:** Framework for post-job review, feedback collection, and continuous improvement.
