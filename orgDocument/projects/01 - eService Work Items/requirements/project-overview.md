# eService Work Items Management System - Project Overview

## Project Identifier
PRJ-01

## Objective
Digitalize and streamline work item management for energy services to improve operational efficiency, reduce manual processes, and enhance service delivery tracking.

## Scope
- Work item creation and management
- Digital workflow automation
- Real-time status tracking
- Integration with field operations
- Reporting and analytics

## Key Stakeholders
- Operations Management
- Field Personnel
- Customer Service
- IT Department

## Success Criteria
- 50% reduction in manual work item processing time
- Real-time visibility into work item status
- Improved customer communication
- Enhanced operational reporting

## Related Organizational Processes
- [Service Delivery Process](../../orgModel/main.md)
- [Operations Management](../../orgModel/main.md)

## Project Timeline
- Phase 1: Requirements and Analysis (Q1 2026)
- Phase 2: System Design and Development (Q2-Q3 2026)
- Phase 3: Testing and Deployment (Q4 2026)
